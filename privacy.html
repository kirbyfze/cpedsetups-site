Privacy Policy — CpedSetups

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Introduction

CpedSetups ("the App") is a video content automation tool that posts short-form video clips to TikTok using the official TikTok Content Posting API. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle data in connection with the App.

2. Data We Collect

The App is operated solely by its developer for a single TikTok account. We do not collect personal data from any third-party end users. The following limited data is handled:

  • TikTok OAuth Tokens: Authentication tokens issued by TikTok are stored locally on the operator's device. They are never transmitted to any server we control.
  • Video Content: Video files prepared for upload are stored locally on the operator's device and sent directly to TikTok via the API.

3. TikTok API Scopes Used

The App requests the following TikTok API permissions:

  • video.upload — to post video content to the account owner's TikTok profile
  • video.publish — to publish uploaded content publicly

No other scopes are requested. No data accessed via these scopes is retained beyond the API transaction.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information of third parties
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or fingerprints
  • No analytics, cookies, or tracking pixels
  • No data from TikTok followers or other users

5. How Data Is Used

OAuth tokens are used exclusively to authenticate API calls to TikTok. Video files are used exclusively for the purpose of publishing content to the operator's TikTok account. No data is sold, licensed, or shared with any third party.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

No personal data is retained by the App. OAuth tokens are stored only on the operator's local device. If you wish to revoke the App's access to your TikTok account, you may do so at any time from TikTok's Security Settings. To request deletion of any data associated with this App, contact us at cpedz@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

7. User Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you may have rights under GDPR or CCPA respectively, including the right to access, correct, or delete personal data. Since the App does not collect personal data from third parties, these rights primarily apply to the operator (account owner). To exercise any rights, contact cpedz@gmail.com.

8. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the TikTok API. TikTok's own Privacy Policy governs how TikTok handles data on their platform: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

9. Security

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the operator's device. We recommend using standard device security practices (screen lock, disk encryption) to protect these credentials.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
