Privacy Policy — CpedSetups

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Overview

CpedSetups ("the App") uploads video content to TikTok using the TikTok Content Posting API. This policy explains what data is handled.

2. Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from end users. The App operates on behalf of a single TikTok account (the owner) only.

3. TikTok API Data

The App uses OAuth tokens provided by TikTok to authenticate API requests. These tokens are stored locally on the operator's device and are never shared with third parties.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No names, emails, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking

5. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the TikTok API. TikTok's own Privacy Policy governs how TikTok handles data: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

6. Data Retention

No user data is retained by the App.
